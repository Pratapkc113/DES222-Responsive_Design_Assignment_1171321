Methodology Note

This visual essay draws on a wide range of sources spanning art, science, technology and critical theory. The selection prioritizes works that bridge these disciplines, particularly those that explore innovative ways of representing and interacting with environmental data. While many specific projects and publications are cited here, the essay is also informed by broader engagement with environmental media studies, data visualization practice, and climate communication research.
